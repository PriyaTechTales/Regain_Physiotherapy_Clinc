Our Services

Comprehensive treatments designed for your specific needs.

Cervical Traction

Gentle stretching of the cervical spine to relieve neck pain and improve mobility.

Lumbar Traction

Mechanical stretching of the lumbar spine to reduce lower back pain and disc pressure.

Short Wave Diathermy (SWD)

Deep heat therapy using electromagnetic waves to reduce pain and inflammation.

TENS

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation for pain relief through electrical impulses.

Muscle Stimulator

Electrical stimulation to strengthen muscles and improve function after injury.

Paraffin Wax Bath

Heat therapy using paraffin wax to soothe joints and reduce stiffness.

Infra Red Rays

Infrared light therapy to promote healing and reduce muscle tension.

Ultra Sonic Therapy

Ultrasound waves to penetrate deep tissues and accelerate healing.

Interferential Therapy

Advanced electrical therapy for deep tissue pain relief and muscle stimulation.

Ankle Exerciser

Specialized equipment for ankle strengthening and range of motion exercises.

Quadriceps Table

Targeted strengthening exercises for quadriceps muscles and knee rehabilitation.

Shoulder Wheel

Shoulder range of motion and strengthening exercises using circular motion.

T Pulley

Pulley system for upper body strengthening and shoulder rehabilitation exercises.

Neuro Rehabilitation

Specialized care for neurological conditions requiring physiotherapy and functional recovery.

Sports Injuries

Targeted rehabilitation for athletes to recover from sports-related injuries and return to peak performance.
